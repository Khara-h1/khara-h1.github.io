Blueberry Mocktail Bellini

Make blueberries a part of your daily routine: Grab a Boost of Blue!

Share

Facebook Twitter Pinterest Email
Prep Time: 5 minutes
Portions: 1
Order ingredients from

Ingredients

  • 1⁄2 cup chilled non-alcoholic champagne or sparkling apple cider
  • 2 tablespoons blueberry simple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon blueberries, for garnish
Order ingredients from

Instructions

  1. To a champagne flute, pour in chilled nonalcoholic champagne or sparkling apple cider. Stir in blueberry simple syrup and top with fresh blueberries.
Fresh

About This Recipe

The Blueberry Mocktail Bellini is bubbly, sweet, and oh-so-easy to mix up for an anytime boost of blue! It combines Blueberry Simple Syrup Mocktail Base with non-alcoholic champagne or sparkling apple cider for a drink that always feels celebratory. Fresh blueberries add an extra pop of flavor and color, too. Cheers to you!
